    About Us

    We are a small team of lifeguards based out of the Fraser Valley who are passionate about water safety and want to make sure that everyone has a safe and fun time in the water. Our lifeguards are all certified by the Lifesaving Society and have years of experience working in public and private pools.

    Services

    • Private Lifeguarding
      • Certified with Lifesaving Society's "National Lifeguard" certification
      • $75 an hour
    • Private Swimming Lessons
      • Certified with Lifesaving Society's "Swim Instructor" certification
      • $75 an hour

    Policies

    1. One lifeguard can guard up to 40 people
    2. Availibility of Lifeguards can vary
    3. Waypoint Aquatics may reject jobs outside of the Fraser Valley area
    4. Fees may be subject to change, depending on location
